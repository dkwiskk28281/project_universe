Gaze into your own universe.

COSMOS is a meditation app that renders an infinite universe in real-time 3D. Tens of thousands of stars, procedurally generated nebulae, and deep-space drone sounds come together to create your personal cosmos.

▸ Real-time 3D Universe
Stars, nebulae, and volumetric clouds built with custom shaders that constantly evolve. The camera drifts gently — all you need to do is watch.

▸ Encounters
Somewhere on Earth, someone else is gazing at the same universe. A luminous being crosses your screen, and the words "life detected" appear. No words are exchanged. You simply feel their presence.

▸ Immersive Sound
Layered soundscape powered by Web Audio API — deep drones, brown noise hum, and metallic pulsar pings create a sense of space. Headphones recommended.

▸ Adaptive Quality
Automatically detects your GPU and adjusts between Low / Medium / High quality. Runs smoothly on any iPhone.

---
Open your universe for 5 minutes before sleep each night.
