# Curriculum

## Stage Model

1. Learning habits.
2. Middle-school math recovery.
3. High-school math.
4. Engineering math.
5. Physics.
6. Chemistry.
7. Materials Science.
8. Semiconductor fundamentals.
9. Thin films.
10. Crystal growth.
11. Epitaxy.
12. Research reading.
13. M.S. preparation.

## MVP Course Spine

The first vertical slice starts with `비율과 가스 유량` because it connects math confidence to a real EPI-adjacent engineering concept without exposing dangerous or proprietary process details.

Starter lessons:

- 비율과 가스 유량.
- 단위 환산 생존력.
- 분수, 소수, 백분율.
- 그래프와 기울기.
- 지수와 로그.
- 평균과 표준편차.
- 원자와 결합.
- 결정 결함.
- EPI 수송과 표면반응.
- 논문 읽기.

## Teaching Order

Every core lesson should follow:

1. Phenomenon.
2. Picture or intuition.
3. Numbers.
4. Formula.
5. EPI field connection.
6. Safety boundary.
7. Check for understanding.
